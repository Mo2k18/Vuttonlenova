About Vaunova

Where it all started.

Bird Johnson and Mohamed Ibrahim opened the very first Vaunova Franchise in 2017 in Jackson, TN. They came up with the idea after seeing that designer clothes are much too expensive for anyone, especially pants and jackets for both men and women. So why not sell them but at an affordable price?

Background.

Bird Johnson and Mohamed Ibrahim are the current owners of the Vaunova. The first store was opened in Jackson, TN.
